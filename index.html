What is SAMADHAN?
Objective: The objective of this nationwide hackathon is to gather the young and innovative minds to establish a community where students from all over the country come up with smart solutions,mostly related to digitalization, and help to improve the functioning of of society as a whole. There are several administrative apps as well as industrial and social innovations that help to improve the quality of life of the people.
Problem statement: Open for all real-world problems that can be solved using Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain/Web3, Cyber Security, and Open Innovation.
